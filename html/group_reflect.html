LOOKING BACK...

Nguyen Ha

In general, I consider myself a vital asset to this team since I am willing to share my thoughts and ideas, reliable, and eager to adapt to new things. However, I was at first a little anxious about working with people coming from various cultures and are not really around my age because, for the best part of the past twelve years, I barely been in a multicultural learning atmosphere. To my surprise, as soon as we set the ball rolling, I learnt that our disparities were definitely our strengths. In the end, we managed to bring our relentless ideas to the table in developing a product that can well-reflect our efforts. Although we stumbled into certain challenges throughout the assignments, we were able to overcome them by sticking to the plans and being crystal-clear and supportive to one another. Our presentation is one of the project's most memorable aspects. It feels great to see how our thoughts came together into a powerful presentation. Our commitment and hard work paid off, thus we received good evaluations from our lecturer and classmates. The amount of time we have together to really complete the project prototype is definitely something I would change, if possible. Looking back, I can see on my end how much I have grown as a team leader and as a team member. It seems that any obstacles can be solved through efficient communication and teamwork, and that diversity can be a significant asset in any group scenario. Joshua and I had haphazard conversations about our different backgrounds, did some language exchange, and we ended up learning much from these. Our team found time to crack jokes and enjoy each other's company despite working on a serious assignment with tight deadlines.

Joshua Hansen

Being an exchange student from Denmark in a group with local students, I was met with many different surprises, challenges and experiences during our collaborative group work. Even though there was an apparent cultural difference between me and the other group members, I was surprised to see how well my group received me both socially and academically and that we were able to produce high quality work whilst being social, sharing experiences and stories from our diverse backgrounds. I want to highlight our academic efficiency with all our assignments and how ambitious and talented my group members are. Initially, I had the most experience at University and past group-working and being a confident student, I was certain to exceed any requirements and expectations. I later found out that all my peers are exceptionally smart, hard working and inspiring to work with, as they show great potential as young and newly enrolled students. I am certain that all of my three group members will succeed beyond expectations in their future ventures and I hope to have contributed culturally and academically to future projects and experiences. Many improvements can be made during our time together, but I specifically want to highlight the need of being critical towards IT and the impact that it can have on us, socially and individually. The IT field tends to see technology as merely a tool to solve problems, which I can only encourage to rethink. IT is a part of our identity, and as culture is a dynamic process, so is the meaning of IT in our society. With that in mind, I am glad to have shared my study-abroad experience with my fellow teammates and wish them all the best in the future. I will always have a social and academic door open for any inquiries that my teammates might seek.

Tram Anh

At first, I was a little bit nervous due to my lack of experience in working on a professional group project for such a long time like this before, and the members of my group were also talented, which accidentally put me under enormous peer pressure. But finally, everything turned out to be completely different from what I imagined: all the members were friendly, contributive, and responsible. If my memory serves me right, I do not think that we have had any struggles in building idea consensus, and work contribution, which has wholly dissipated the fear of team working in me. Besides my 10 out of 10 teammates, I realised I had learned many things from this team project. I am inspired and motivated by my talented teammates and the IT professional I worked with during the second assignment. Compared to myself in the past, I believe that I have improved my communication ability, thanks to my amiable and reliable teammates. Additionally, I gained knowledge and experience about the presentation, even though this one may not be my best performance, it helped a lot with the fear of public speaking. In conclusion, there are still a few shortcomings that need to be overcome, but I would love to emphasise the industriousness, harmony, and understanding that we had while working together in the last semester.

Anh Huy

Throughout the team project, there have been lots of up and down moments, but overall, I felt like this is a great chance for me to understand more about the people surrounding me that I am working with and bonding all of us together more. Firstly about our happy moment, it felt like we are a very good team where all of the members contributed to the project through multiple ways. For example, Joshua and Ha were really creative and have lots of great ideas of how we can build the project. Not only that, but they also shared their thoughts a lot and supported any member that is facing difficulty. Tram Anh is a diligent person when she helps the team to build up the report, sharing the task of which person to do which section. While with me, I devoted myself to do the website for the team and use my knowledge to help the team build an amazing website. About our lowest moment, we found out the most difficult part is to get everyone on the same page on how to code, and using their knowledge from the course such as GitHub and HTML, CSS and JS to build the website. Even though there might be a bit of technical gaps, we successfully overcame it. In the end, the things that make me most surprised is how people are very diligent and work hard for this assignment but also they put lots of effort for each of the components in the assignment like the report, presentation and even the website. One thing that I learnt from this team project is that everyone will have their strengths and weaknesses, and we need to work as a team, support each other and know how to make others expose their strengths. With only that, we can all perform at our best.

To sum up our experience...

Essentials of Information Technology (IT) is the course that provides the foundation knowledge of contemporary software, applications, and job skills required to enter the tech market. The knowledge that we gained from this course was beneficial in supporting us to have a more general outlook into the IT industry, as well as helping us to form our future occupations. During this course, we had a major group project assignment, which created the opportunity for us to meet up and work together. The project requirements were to develop a product using IT technologies as much as possible in the time available. To be frank, the teamwork beyond our group has been even greater than we expected. Every task has been evenly distributed without any arguments and done carefully, nobody procrastinated or missed any deadlines, even if two among the members in our group took four courses this semester. The other thing we love about our group is that we could comfortably share our ideas, and the rest of the group would be all ears, trying to comprehend and develop the ideas together, everyone’s attitude towards the project were professional and in tune at the same time, which made our group become so amazing. Thanks to the vigour the members put into this project, everything has been done smoothly barely without any obstacles. The team was even cooler than expected, to the extent that if someone asked: ‘What could have been improved in this project?’, we would say that we wish we could spend more time together, not only because of the project but also because of the knowledge that we shared and learnt from each other. We have had the chance to team up with incredible people, who gain both experience in the IT industry and good personal qualities, which motivated us to push ourselves to the limits. In conclusion, this project is an amazing opportunity for us to learn and gain more experience in early project management, soft skills, such as communication, critical thinking, and teamwork. Moreover, we have started a wonderful friendship with one another throughout the course.
